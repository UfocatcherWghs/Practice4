Gloom Hops

About the game

The main character, Blobby lives in an abusive family where his parents constantly fought in front of him and his younger sister. He is sick of all of the chaos and drama, and all he wanted to do is give his sister a normal life. So he sets off on a journey to get a stable job and get money. He left to go to a large city. Since he is a foreigner and lacks expierience. Then he stumbles upon a gang and he joins them since throughout this process he wasn't feeling very good about himself. To prove his worth he needs to steal money. For Blobby this is seems like the only option he has to help his little sister. So he joins. Later it's not really about his sister, and he gets more greedy for money. Will you help Blobby?

Game play

You will play as Blobby. The levels increase in difficulty as you progress. Guide Blobby to jump at the right time to collect coins and complete the level. There are 3 worlds and multiple levels.

Made by Shaivi & Tiffany 2023 for a CDT class project.
